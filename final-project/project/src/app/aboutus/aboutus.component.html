At CityBarks Adoption, we're dedicated to improving the world, one paw at a time. Founded by compassionate city veterinarians, our commitment to securing loving homes for dogs in need knows no bounds. Our journey began with a simple yet profound idea: every dog deserves a forever family.

Our team of passionate volunteers, driven by a shared love for our four-legged companions, works tirelessly day in and day out. We are resolute in our mission to rescue, rehabilitate, and rehome dogs of all breeds and backgrounds. We firmly believe that with the right care, the support of our community, and a nurturing environment, every dog can not only survive but truly thrive.

Through dog care and outreach programs, we aim to create a world where every dog is cherished, and where no dog is left behind.
